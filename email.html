Hi all,
 
Today, we are launching Keep Texas Together, a campaign grounded in a simple truth: immigrants strengthen Texas. Immigrants have shaped our communities for generations — in our schools, businesses, hospitals, congregations, and neighborhoods. We work, live, play, and care for our communities together. We are all Texans.
 
Immigrants aren’t the threat — they’re the thread.
 
 
But fear-based narratives have taken hold, causing some Texans to lose sight of what binds us together. As people rooted in our neighborhoods are treated like outsiders and torn from our communities, the threads that have connected us for generations are beginning to unravel.
 
Keep Texas Together aims to change that. It uplifts the values that have always made our communities strong as Texans: hard work, family, and the way people show up for one another.
 
Please explore and share the campaign resources linked below.

Thank you for your partnership and for helping ensure people across Texas hear the full story of who we are and what strengthens our communities.
